Product Features




Auto Resume Parsing

Our parser is highly accurate making it one of the best products in the market. This is backed up by Continuous Research, Benchmarked AI algorithms and Machine Learning bettering everyday.


Auto JD Parsing

Teagle is linked to all channels such as social, job boards, company database, email, etc. On receipt of the JD it automatically searches and brings out the best profiles continuing this until the position closed.


Matching & Ranking

Our unique ontology model combines with AI algorithms enables the match accurately and ranks the candidates with the ranking to make it easy to take action.




Database Refresh

Teagle keeps reaching out to passive candidates to keep the database updated on a periodical basis as per settings. This will ensure to find the candidates sooner and reducing the cost of hire.


Auto Rematch

On receiving new JD the system automatically first searches the internal database to find the right match. Teagle keeps record opf past matches and their status to ensure only the right candidates are reach out.


24/7 Virtual Assistant

Our Virtual Assistant works for you 24/7 by filtering, finding and reaching out the right candidates and reminding recruiters to take actions on time without missing any good candidates.




Candidate Engagement

Teagle reaches out to matched candidates to do the initial interest check and collect basic information so that the initial screening is completed reducing recruiter's time tremendously.


Matching Trends

Teagle keeps a watch on the matching trends on how the hiring managers and candidates are matched for which job thereby providing higher accuracy of future matches.
